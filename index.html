Classic Margherita Pizza

1 hour 30 mins Medium 4 servings

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. 1

    Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let stand for 5 minutes until foamy.

  2. 2

    Combine flour and salt in a large bowl. Add yeast mixture and olive oil.

  3. 3

    Knead dough for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

  4. 4

    Place in oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour.

  5. 5

    Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C) with pizza stone if available.

  6. 6

    Roll out dough and transfer to baking sheet.

  7. 7

    Spread tomato sauce, add sliced mozzarella.

  8. 8

    Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.

  9. 9

    Top with fresh basil leaves and serve hot.
